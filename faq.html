I think you should choose me because of my passion for computer science. I love programming due to it's freedom to express my artistic ability without having toy draw anything. I just love how powerful programming can be.

What are your goals?

My goals are to graduate college, get a job, make some money, and most importantly be happy. I want to make those around me proud. I want to have financial stability, that way I minimize the stress in my life. I want to better myself as a person. I want to be able to help those around me.

I learned to do this all in Web Development or ITIS 3135. I also learned some more of the stuff on my own. Google is really the most powerful tool when wanting to learn to program. I also enjoy watching videos since they are visual and walk you through. I am not much of a reader so videos are a helpful and powerful alternative.

Why is there a NHL Scoreboard on the Homepage?

The scoreboard is just there to exercise my knowledge in JSON API and AJAX requests. I have already had experience with the NHL API so I thought making a scoreboard would be a perfect idea. I also made the scoreboard itself using photoshop.
